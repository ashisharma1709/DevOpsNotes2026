🚀 Create AWS Free Tier Account
Steps

🔹1 Navigate to AWS Free Tier page
Go to: https://aws.amazon.com/free/

🔹2️.Start signup
Click on Create Free Account → you will be redirected to the signup page:
https://signin.aws.amazon.com/signup?request_type=register

🔹3. Enter email and account name
Provide your Email ID and AWS account name (e.g., ashish29Apr2025), then click on Verify email address, enter the captcha, and submit.

🔹4. Verify email
Enter the verification code received on your email and click on Verify.

🔹5. Set root user password 🔐
Create a root user password and enter the same in confirm password.
👉 Make sure to save or remember it securely.

🔹6.Select plan
Choose the Free 6-month plan.

🔹7️.Enter contact information 📇
Select Personal (for your own projects)
Enter Full Name, Mobile Number, Address
Tick the checkbox and click on Agree and Continue


🔹8️. Enter billing information 💳
Provide your Credit Card details:

Card Number
Expiry Date
CVV
Cardholder Name
Then click on Continue


🔹9️. OTP verification 📱
Enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number (linked to the credit card) and submit.
👉 INR 2.00 will be deducted for verification


🔹10. Confirm your identity 🆔
Select Personal Use
Ownership type: Individual
Document: PAN Card
Enter DOB and PAN Number
Upload PAN card document
Click on Continue


🔹11. Complete signup & activate account ✅
AWS will process your details (this may take a few minutes).
Once verification is successful, your account will be created.


Login to AWS Console 🔑  https://signin.aws.amazon.com/ 

Login using:
Root user (Email ID)
Password (same as used during signup process)


🔥 Optional 

🔐 12. Secure your account (Important)
Enable MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) for root user
Create an IAM admin user
Avoid using root user for daily tasks


- AWS Console: https://aws.amazon.com/console/  
- Sign-in Page: https://signin.aws.amazon.com/  
- IAM Login URL: https://<your-alias>.signin.aws.amazon.com/console  
- AWS Management Console: https://console.aws.amazon.com/
